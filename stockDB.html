Opes Capital

Can generative transformers provide flexibility and insight that current regression cannot? I created a backtesting system and database to test this.

Opes Capital was interested in training a generative transformer that would help narrow down investment options and provide advice for 6- and 12-month trading. To set up the infratstructure for this, I was in charge of designing and implementing a database structure to most efficiently access data as well as building a backtester to analyze the success of any transformer they trained. Next semester, I may be working on the transformer itself.

The database

For this project, I consulted with a google cloud expert who helped me figure out the general structure that would work best for my specific needs. As the project grew and my understanding of what was nessesary expanded, I went through multiple iterations of designs. In addition to design, I had to clean input data (about 8000 companies worth), merge multiple entries for the same companies, and generate meaningful metadata for each company.
